Our Story

Born from Passion, Built on Tradition

GnanaBarani Furnitures was founded with a single vision — to bring the timeless beauty of handcrafted South Indian furniture to every home. Based in Viluppuram, Tamil Nadu, we started as a small workshop and have grown into one of the region's most trusted names in premium wooden furniture.

Every piece we create tells a story. Our master craftsmen, trained through generations of carpentry tradition, transform raw teak and rosewood into functional works of art. From the intricate carvings on a wardrobe door to the smooth polish of a diwan bench — every detail is crafted with care.

We serve families across Viluppuram, Cuddalore, Pondicherry, Chennai and beyond, delivering furniture that becomes a cherished part of your home for decades.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Three pillars that define everything we do at GnanaBarani Furnitures.

Uncompromising Quality

We use only premium grade teak and rosewood sourced responsibly. Every joint, finish and carving is inspected before a piece leaves our workshop. We don't cut corners — ever.

Honesty & Trust

We believe in transparent pricing and honest communication. What you see is what you get — no hidden charges, no compromises on materials, and delivery on time, every time.

Affordable Excellence

Because we build everything in our own workshop, we eliminate middlemen and pass the savings to you. Premium handcrafted furniture shouldn't break the bank — and with us, it won't.

Our Workshop & Showroom

Where Wood Becomes Art

Our workshop at No. 20 Varatharajan Layout, Viluppuram is where the magic happens. Our skilled artisans work with traditional tools and techniques passed down through generations, combined with modern precision for a flawless finish.

You are welcome to visit our showroom to see, touch and experience the quality firsthand. Our team will help you choose the right piece for your home — or work with you on a custom design.
